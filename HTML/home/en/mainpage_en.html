Ping's Pet Shop is a pet and animal needs shop that opened its doors in Turku at the turn of the year..
We specialize in the sale of rodents, fish and terrario animals.
We will also find supplies and food for your pets, including cats and dogs.
In our selection you will also find food insects.
Our services include a nail cutting service for dogs, guinea pigs and rabbits.
In addition, it is possible to bring your pet to holiday care for us. Read more about our services.
